Classic Creamy Pasta

A rich and creamy Italian pasta recipe perfect for cozy dinners.

⏱ Prep Time: 15 mins

🔥 Cook Time: 20 mins

🍽 Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup cream
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil and add cream in a pan.
  3. Mix in cheese and stir well.
  4. Combine pasta with sauce, season with salt & pepper.
  5. Garnish with basil and serve hot.

Tips & Nutrition

💡 Add grilled chicken or mushrooms for extra flavor. Each serving has approx. 420 kcal.
